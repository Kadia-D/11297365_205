Mission & Vision:

To promote and protect the positive reputation of Computer Science students and transform them into problem-solvers after school.


The association was formed to address student issues and foster a stronger bond among the members of the department.

This is achieved through it various activities such as:

Hackathons

All- night hackathons are periodically organised for students to showcase their talents and network.

Monthly challenges

Occasssionally, the associations puts out fun questions online for students to answer, such as favorite programming language, most hated course,etc. to know more about each other.

ICT competitions

The associated introduces its members to interational competitions,such as the Huawei competition,which could be a huge stepping stone for their career.

Updates

The association also regularly updates members on internships and job opportunities in tech.

Dinners & Trips

COMPSSA also organises magnificent dinners and exciting tours for members to enjoy and release the stress and burden of the semester
